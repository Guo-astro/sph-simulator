## üîç How to Obtain Papers

### Option 1: University/Research Institution Access
Most universities provide access to these journals through their library subscriptions:
- JSTOR
- ScienceDirect
- Oxford Academic
- IOP Science
- SpringerLink
- Wiley Online Library

### Option 2: ResearchGate & Academia.edu
Many authors upload preprints or accepted manuscripts:
1. Go to https://www.researchgate.net
2. Search for the paper title and authors
3. Look for "Request full-text PDF" or direct download links

### Option 3: Google Scholar
1. Go to https://scholar.google.com
2. Search for the paper title
3. Click on the paper title
4. Look for "All X versions" or direct PDF links
5. Check if your institution provides access

### Option 4: arXiv Preprints
Some papers have preprints available:
- Search https://arxiv.org for the title
- Look in astrophysics sections (astro-ph)

### Option 5: Author Websites
Some authors provide PDFs on their personal websites:
- Search for "[Author Name] [University] website"
- Check publications/research pages
